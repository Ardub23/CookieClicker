Ardub's Cookie Clicker mods

Seed Seek

Current version: 1.0.1 (changelog)

This mod replaces the "Garden info" tooltip's tutorial with a list of all the plants you can get by mutation of the seeds you already have. Much easier than reading tables and flowcharts!

Auto JQB

Current version: 0.2 (WIP)

This mod adds options to automate the process of farming juicy queenbeets, including:

  • Planting queenbeets when the garden is empty
  • Automatically save-scumming for juicy queenbeets to appear when possible
  • Planting elderworts to speed up juicy queenbeet growth
  • Save-scumming to ensure that at least one juicy queenbeet ages with each tick
  • Harvesting juicy queenbeets once they're mature

This mod will only work if your farms are level 9 or higher. Before then, all automation features will be inactive. (Automation will also be deactivated if your garden is frozen, or if it's occupied with something other than juicy queenbeet farming.)

Disable Big Cookie

Current version: 1.0.1 (changelog)

Literally just adds an option to prevent the big cookie from being clicked. Nice if you want to protect your shimmering veil or go for True Neverclick.
