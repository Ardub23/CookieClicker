Ardub's Cookie Clicker mods

Seed Seek

Current version: 1.0.12 (changelog)

Replaces the "Garden info" tooltip's tutorial with a list of all the plants you can get by mutation of the seeds you already have. Much easier than reading tables and flowcharts!

Auto JQB

Current version: 1.1.9 (changelog)

Adds options to automate the process of farming juicy queenbeets, including:

  • Planting queenbeets when the garden is empty
  • Automatically save-scumming for juicy queenbeets to appear when possible
  • Planting elderworts to speed up juicy queenbeet growth
  • Save-scumming to ensure that juicy queenbeets age with each tick
  • Harvesting juicy queenbeets once they're mature

All options are disabled by default; enable them in the options menu. Automation is also deactivated when your garden is frozen or occupied with something other than juicy queenbeet farming.

As an alternative to this mod, you might like the Auto Sacrifice mod by LabyrinthX.

Disable Big Cookie

Current version: 1.0.10 (changelog)

Literally just adds an option to prevent the big cookie from being clicked. Nice if you want to protect your shimmering veil or go for True Neverclick.

Full-Size Golden Cookies

Current version: 1.0.0

Prevents the "fancy graphics" option from making golden cookies smaller.

These mods are not maintained as actively as they once were, but you can still report an issue on GitHub (or submit a pull request).
